    All about Ashlar

    This Role Playing Game is based around the world of Ashlar where you play as your choice of race,
    gender, and class. The main objective of the game is to survive as many waves as you can,
    these waves containing monsters, monsters and more monsters.

    Hurrah!

    Members of the
    Ashlar Creators

    The brains and leader of the Ashlar group is Nathan Hui. Samuel Luk, our creative and innovative graphic designer. Anthony Siu, our manager, making sure everything works fine. And lastly, Patrick Hanlon, our Co-producer (helps with code).

    How Ashlar
    came to be

    Us four decided to sign up for a programming course over a week's period of time. Having learned a thing or two from the course, we decided to fufill our life-long dream, to create a game! Thus Ashlar was born.
